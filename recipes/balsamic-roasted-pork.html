Balsamic Roasted Pork Loin

Description

A zesty, yet simple meal with an olive oil and balsamic vinegar marinade. A crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons steak seasoning rub
  • ½ cup balsamic vinegar
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 2 pounds boneless pork loin roast

Steps

  1. Dissolve steak seasoning in balsamic vinegar, then stir in olive oil.
  2. Place pork into a resealable plastic bag and pour marinade overtop. Squeeze out air and seal bag; marinate 2 hours to overnight.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Place pork into a glass baking dish along with marinade.
  5. Bake in preheated oven, basting occasionally until the pork reaches an internal temperature of 145 degrees F (65 degrees C), about 1 hour.
  6. Let the roast rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
